Why a Saudi-Specific DevOps Practice

DevOps in Saudi Arabia is different from DevOps in London or San Francisco. The Kingdom’s Vision 2030 programme is driving digital transformation at a pace that creates unique challenges: NEOM and giga-project teams scaling from 10 to 500+ engineers in 18 months, SAMA-regulated fintechs needing compliant-by-design pipelines, NCA Essential Cybersecurity Controls adding security gates to every deployment, and PDPL data residency requiring Saudi-hosted infrastructure.

Platform engineering Riyadh teams need must account for these realities. Our consultants understand the Saudi regulatory context, the AWS Middle East (Riyadh) infrastructure landscape, and the specific talent dynamics - Iqama timelines, Nitaqat ratios, and NEOM/Aramco competing for the same DevOps engineers - that make building DevOps capability in the Kingdom uniquely challenging.
